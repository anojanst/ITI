Affiliations and Associations

University of the Sunshine Coast (USC), Australia

International Training Institute is associated with the University of the Sunshine Coast. The ITI New Diploma courses can obtain 4 credits at USC.

Information in relation to USC can be accessed at http://www.usc.edu.au/

PNG Human Resource Institute

International Training Institute has arrangement with the PNGHRI under which ITI Human Resource Students will become student members of Papua New Guinea Human Resource Institute.

University of Southern Queensland (USQ)

International Training Institute is associated with the University of Southern Queensland (USQ). Currently ITI is an agent for USQ. The ITI Advanced Diploma in Accounting graduates can obtain credits of 4 core units and 4 minor units for business degrees at USQ.

For the ITI Diploma in Business courses, credits for 4 units can be attained for the business degrees offered at USQ. Information in relation to USQ can be accessed at www.usq.edu.au

Griffith University, Australia

International Training Institute is associated with the Griffith University of Queensland. The ITI New Diploma courses can obtain 8 credits at Griffith University.

Information in relation to Griffith University can be accessed at https://www.griffith.edu.au

Association of Accounting Technicians (AAT) of Australia

AAT Australia is the institution that International Training Institute (ITI) has strategically aligned itself – for the benefit of its students and Accounting Technicians employed in all sectors of the economy here in Papua New Guinea. As of end of 2007 AAT has been recognised by the following institutions as para-professional qualification:

• The Institute of Chartered Accountants in Australia (ICAA)

• National Institute of Accountants (NIA)

• CPA Australia

Certified Practicing Accountants of PNG (CPA PNG)

The International Training Institute has an association with the Certified Practicing Accountants of Papua New Guinea (CPA PNG). Students who have successfully completed the course Advanced Diploma in Accounting wish to become Certified Accounting Technicians Members (CAT).

ITI Business Studies Students can become Students members with CPA Papua New Guinea.

International Federation of Training & Development Organization (IFTDO)

The International Federation of Training & Development Organisation (IFTDO) has granted International Training Institute membership to its organisation. IFTDO allows ITI to be recognised as part of the international network of professionals with 500, 000 members in 50 countries. ITI as a member has the opportunity to;

• Increase recognition as an internationally active organisation by using the IFTDO logo

• Expand capabilities through exchanging strategies, techniques and technologies.

• Learn about important HR events and

• Access world thinking and practices through the IFTDO global network, project and research

• Shape state-of-the-art international development projects

• Participate as delegates in the IFTDO conferences

• Contribute articles in the quarterly IFTDO News and website

Australian College of Business & Management (ACBM)

ITI is pleased to announce that it has partnered with the Australian College of Business & Management (ACBM) in order to extend the range and scope of our training offerings in Papua New Guinea.

All ITI/ACBM training programs are designed to:

• Provide maximum industry relevant, applied training

• Focus on developing business-ready, employable skills

• Establish improved career opportunities

International Education Pathways & Program (IEPP)

Providing PNG Students with Affordable, World Class Education Opportunities at Acharya Institutes, Bangalore, India. IEPP (PNG) is a partnership between the International Training Institute (ITI) of Port Moresby and IEPP Pty. Ltd. (Australia), the regional lead Agent and Representative of the Acharya Group of Institutions, India. IEPP (PNG) has been created to open up to PNG partnership with the Acharya Group of Institutions (www.acahrya.ac.in), IEPP is able to provide a fully comprehensive service which promotes and facilitates educational programs for PNG students – at pre-university, undergraduate and postgraduate levels – in a broad range of study areas including: Engineering, Management, Business Studies, Computer Applications, Arts and Sciences, Pharmacy, Nursing, Teacher Education, Fashion & Design, Architecture as well as Pre-University Foundation Studies.

James Cook University, Australia

International Training Institute is associated with the James Cook University (JCU), Australia.

James Cook University is the second oldest university in Queensland, recognised in the higher education sector for achievements in teaching and research. Recently ranked by Times Higher Education (THE) in the ‘Top 150 Universities under 50’ (2016), James Cook University continues to deliver high quality degrees preparing students for entry into the global workforce. Students who choose to study at JCU Brisbane will walk away with a tertiary qualification from a respected and highly acclaimed university. In addition to this, students will develop connections with a world renowned business, recognised for achievements in vocational and tertiary education, training, employment, recruitment and apprenticeships.

JCU Brisbane has a 24 subject Bachelor of Business programs and the following credits are recognised from ITI.

The credits are below,

Current Diploma:

· Diploma in Accounting, HR, Marketing and Management 5 subject credits

New Diploma (2 years):

· Diploma in Accounting, HR, Marketing and management 8 subject credits

· Diploma in IT 4 subjects

Information in relation to JCU can be accessed at https://www.jcu.edu.au/
